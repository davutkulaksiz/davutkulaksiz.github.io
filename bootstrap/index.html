Having anxiety is a full time job you don't get paid for.


It's an endless cycle that contains constant thinking, planning, problem solving and worrying. It's tiring and taxing on the body. You are on the edge 24/7.

It makes you feel like you are missing out on life.


When you are living in the bubble of anxiety and dread, you'll never fully enjoy yourself at the moment. You can't live presently.

Do not suffer twice. Pet a cat.


Owning any pet is good for your heart. Cats in particular lower your stress level—possibly since they don’t require as much effort as dogs—and lower the amount of anxiety in your life. Petting a cat has a positive calming effect.
